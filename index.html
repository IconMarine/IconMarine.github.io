About Us

Icon Marine is a local and owner operated, marine transportation company, providing launch boat services to the Port of Vancouver. We strive to provide the safest, most reliable, and immediate form of transport services to our users. Icon Marine will not only service your needs with prompt and dependable service, but offer it transparently, and at the most economical price point.

Our Services

Icon Marine provides safe, reliable, and timely launch boat services.

  • Transportation of personnel and supplies to and from ships (agents / inspectors / surveyors / owners representatives, Transport Canada, CBSA)

  • Delivery of ship’s stores, provisions, and equipment to vessels at anchor, or alongside

  • Water taxi services

Our Boats

We operate the absolute toughest, most dependable, welded aluminum boats, specifically designed for our Coast. Our vessels have capacity for 12, and are certified, documented, and inspected by Transport Canada. Each boat is powered by Volvo Penta. Our clean diesels offer top performance, but most importantly are good for the environment, offering the highest standard of fuel efficiency. These boats are here to serve YOU!
